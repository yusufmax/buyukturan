About the Company

Built on
Purpose

Great Turan Defence was founded to address a growing need: reliable, locally engineered protective equipment for military and security personnel operating in demanding conditions.

Drawing on the symbolism of the Turanian wolf — vigilant, resilient, and protective by nature — our company is built around a single mission: to design and manufacture defence equipment that performs without compromise.

Headquartered in Tashkent, Uzbekistan, we combine modern materials engineering with strict adherence to defence-grade safety standards, positioning ourselves as a long-term, trusted partner for institutional and governmental clients.
